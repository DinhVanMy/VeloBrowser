Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 27, 2025

Velo Browser does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data whatsoever. Your browsing activity stays entirely on your device.

Data Collection

Velo Browser collects zero data. We do not use analytics services, crash reporting tools, or any form of telemetry. Specifically:

  • No browsing history is sent to any server
  • No search queries are logged or tracked
  • No personal information is collected
  • No cookies or identifiers are transmitted to us
  • No third-party analytics SDKs are included
  • No advertising identifiers are accessed

Local Data Storage

Velo Browser stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Bookmarks — Saved web page URLs and titles
  • Browsing History — URLs and titles of visited pages (not recorded in Private Browsing mode)
  • Downloaded Files — Files you choose to download from the web
  • Settings — Your preferences (search engine choice, ad blocker state)
  • Website Data — Cookies and cache stored by WKWebView for visited websites

All local data can be cleared at any time from Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data.

Ad Blocker

Velo Browser includes a built-in ad blocker that uses static, embedded filter rules. These rules are bundled with the app and are not downloaded from any external server. The ad blocker operates entirely on-device using Apple's WebKit Content Rule List API.

Private Browsing

When using Private Browsing mode, Velo Browser uses a non-persistent data store. No history, cookies, or website data from private tabs is saved to your device.

Background Audio & Picture-in-Picture

Velo Browser can play media in the background and in Picture-in-Picture mode. Media playback is handled entirely on-device. No media usage data is collected or transmitted.

Network Requests

The only network requests Velo Browser makes are those initiated by you — loading web pages, downloading files, and playing media. The app itself makes no requests to our servers or any third-party services.

Third-Party Services

Velo Browser uses no third-party libraries, SDKs, or services. The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks (WebKit, AVFoundation, SwiftUI, SwiftData).

Children's Privacy

Velo Browser does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we collect no data from any users, this applies equally to users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above and include the updated policy in the next app update.
